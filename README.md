Awareness of phishing tactics and email threat analysis skills. 

# Task 01 - Phishing Email Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this task is to examine a phishing email and identify common indicators that show it is fraudulent. This includes checking the sender's email address, email headers, links, language used, and other suspicious characteristics.

## Tools Used

- Web Browser
- Online Email Header Analyzer
- Markdown

## Files Included

- `sample_email.txt` – Sample phishing email used for analysis.
- `analysis.md` – Detailed observations and findings.
- `screenshots/` – Screenshots of the sample email and header analysis.

## Outcome

After completing this task, I was able to identify multiple phishing indicators such as a spoofed sender address, suspicious links, urgent language, and inconsistencies in the email header. This exercise helped me understand how phishing emails are designed to trick users.
